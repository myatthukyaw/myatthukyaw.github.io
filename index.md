## Projects

### 🔬 YOLO-Distill
`PyTorch` · `YOLOv9` · `Knowledge Distillation`

Knowledge distillation for YOLOv9 object detection models. Implements **Channel-Wise Distillation (CWD)** and **Masked Generative Distillation (MGD)** to transfer intermediate feature knowledge from a larger teacher model to a smaller student model — achieving model compression with minimal accuracy loss.

[**→ View Repository**](https://github.com/myatthukyaw/yolo-distill)

### 🚗 Ego-Vision
`YOLOv11` · `ByteTrack` · `Depth Anything V2`

A real-time driving scene understanding system for dashcam footage. Combines **YOLOv11** (object detection), **ByteTrack** (tracking), and **Depth Anything V2** (depth estimation) to classify scenarios into driving commands — `GO`, `SLOW DOWN`, `STOP`, or `EMERGENCY BRAKE` — overlaid live on the video stream.
